About Us ▲

Urgent Mindset, Inc. is a healthcare consulting and operations management group built to support providers, agencies, and health systems through complex transitions, compliance demands, and organizational growth. We serve as an extension of your team—offering expert-led solutions, staffing, and long-term strategic insight to improve care delivery and operational health.

We are passionate about designing sustainable, people-centered solutions in a healthcare environment that is constantly evolving.

Our Services ▲

Our core services include the following, and each is tailored to meet the specific needs of healthcare providers, behavioral health organizations, and care delivery systems:

Healthcare Staffing Solutions

We provide healthcare organizations with highly qualified clinical and administrative professionals who are ready to deliver. Whether short-term staffing support or long-term placement is needed, our team matches the right talent with the right mission—ensuring seamless continuity of care and operations.

Compliance Audits and Readiness Reviews

Our audit support goes beyond box-checking. We conduct in-depth compliance reviews, risk assessments, and mock audits to prepare your organization for state, federal, and payer oversight. We help you find the gaps before the regulators do—and build corrective action plans that work.

RFP Response Development

We offer full-service support in writing and managing competitive responses to RFPs, grants, and contracts. From strategy to submission, we craft proposals that reflect your strengths, align with funder priorities, and give you the best chance at winning.

Program Development & Implementation

From concept to launch, we help organizations design, pilot, and scale new programs that meet community needs and funding requirements. Our team handles all elements of development—from regulatory compliance to staffing and workflows—so your vision becomes a reality.

Staff Training & Professional Development

We offer customized training modules and coaching that equip your workforce with the tools they need to succeed. Whether clinical compliance, cultural competency, or leadership training, we provide upskilling that's relevant, engaging, and rooted in real world practice.

Accreditation Preparation

Achieving accreditation is a milestone—and we're here to walk you through every step. We guide organizations through accreditation preparation for bodies like CARF, The Joint Commission, NCQA, and others. From documentation to practice readiness, we ensure you're not just prepared—you're confident.

Operations Management

Our consultants offer short- and long-term support for organizational operations, including workflow optimization, strategic planning, and crisis response. We help you identify inefficiencies, stabilize core systems, and build sustainable infrastructure for future growth.

Clinical Research

We support healthcare organizations in developing and operationalizing clinical research programs, with a focus on site readiness, sponsor engagement, and regulatory compliance. Our services include feasibility assessments, Principal Investigator alignment, study startup processes, and infrastructure development for Phase II–IV trials. We work closely with leadership and clinical teams to ensure research integration aligns with existing service lines while meeting sponsor and IRB expectations.

Quality Assurance

We develop and implement Quality Assurance and Performance Improvement (QAPI) systems tailored to healthcare and behavioral health settings. This includes audit readiness preparation, documentation review processes, internal monitoring tools, and corrective action planning. We ensure organizations maintain continuous compliance while using data to drive measurable improvements in service delivery.
